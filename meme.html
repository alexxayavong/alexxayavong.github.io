Meme Mashup

For my meme mashup, I used three different memes. There's the cryig Michael Jordan meme, the "We Are Number One" meme, and the meme where America becomes the number one country to have COVID-19 cases. I wanted to show how America, being the one country that always wants to be number one, has achieved a new achievement. But this achievement is an achievement that we shouldn't be too proud of.
